AppSales

Welcome! AppSales is the premier tool to download and analyze your App Store sales reports from iTunes Connect directly on your iPhone.

A few things to note:
AppSales downloads the reports from iTunes Connect directly from the website at https://itts.apple.com. If this website changes significantly, AppSales might not work properly. In this case, we'll try to provide an update ASAP. Please feel free to notify us of any bugs or strange behaviour you notice at http://code.google.com/p/appsales-mobile/.

AppSales downloads currency exchange rates from the web to display your revenue in local currency. Please note that the displayed (current) exchange rates might not reflect your actual payout on the day your money is transferred. It is meant as a rough guideline.

The reports you'll see in AppSales are to be considered as trend reports. Please do NOT regard them as a substitute to your monthly royalty reports that you can download at http://itunesconnect.apple.com. We do NOT guarantee that either the reports or the displayed currency rates are accurate. Please download backup copies of the original report files on your computer.
